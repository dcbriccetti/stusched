About This System

One of the challenges in teaching programming to kids is organizing the kids into groups by ability, interest, budget, and availability. There are many students who would be taking classes with me now if only they could join with enough suitable classmates to form a class. I am developing this system to help with that.

It will take a bit of effort, but if you add yourself and your young programmers to this system and keep the information up to date, you are much more likely to have the classes you want very soon. The approach of me guessing at what people want and when they want it hasn’t worked very well.

With this system you can:

  • Register yourself as the parent or guardian of one or more students interested in learning computer programming from me
  • Provide information about each of your children you are seeking instruction for
  • Enroll your children in classes
  • Browse available courses and sections (instances of courses)
  • See how many students are interested in each course
  • See the history of your children’s attendance in course sections
  • Propose courses

Soon you will be able to:

  • Propose course sections to help form groups

This is free software. The source code is here.
